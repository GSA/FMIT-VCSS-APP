Dispute Statement or Details

To dispute a statement, or one or more details of a statement, select Dispute Statement/Details.

 

 

To dispute the entire Statement

 

  • Click "Dispute Entire Statement".
  • Click Next.
  • Enter the required information (marked with a red asterisk).
  • Attach supporting documentation if desired.
  • Click Next.
  • Review the data entered.  Click Back if you need to change anything.
  • Click Next or add attachments if desired.
  • Click "Submit Dispute".

 

To dispute selected details

 

  • Click "Choose Which Detail Records to Dispute".
  • Click "Next".
  • Enter search criteria if desired.
  • Click Search.
  • Sort the list if desired.  
  • The list of line items may be several pages long.  For each page:
    • Click the check box next to any line items to be disputed.
    • Click Mark for Dispute.
  • Click "Next".
  • Enter the required fields and click next.
  • Review the data presented on the next page, and click Next.
  • Review the list of detailed lines to dispute on the next page.  
  • Click a selected item and then "Remove Details" if an item was selected in error.
  • Click "Submit Dispute Request" to send the dispute request to the GSA.
