Business Line Summary

The Business Line Summary provides a total by Business Line within Account Code.   

 

 

On the Business Line Summary page, enter search criteria and click the search button.

 

  • The statement date range is required and must be less than one year.

 

The search results field will be filled with a list of the account code / business line combinations that meet the search criteria entered.   The following options are then available::

 

  • Download the search results to a CSV file.
  • Sort the search results.
  • Select a line item and click "View Statements" to jump to the Statement Search screen.  
  • Select a line item and click the "View Payments" button to jump to the Customer Payment Search screen.
