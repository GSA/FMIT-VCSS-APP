How does someone pay an organization's bill?

IPAC bills are processed by the GSA through the IPAC system.  Payment may still be made via IPAC for a non-IPAC statement, of course, and the GSA's IPAC data is found on the statement.  Alternatively, customer organizations may send a check to the remit to address on the statement.  If the latter method is selected, remember to include the statement number on the check to facilitate processing of the payment.
