How does a user contact the GSA?

Users who can log in to VCSS should

 

  • Use the "Create Account Correspondence" feature to contact the GSA about issues with a customer account code or about general questions not involving a statement.  

  • Use the "Create Statement Correspondence" feature to contact the GSA about issues pertaining to an individual statement or its details.
     

Users who cannot log in should see the "contact us" tab at http://vcss.gsa.gov for alternative contact methods.
