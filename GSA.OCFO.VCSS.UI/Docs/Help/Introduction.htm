Vendor and Customer Self Service (VCSS) System - Customer Information

 

 

The VCSS system provides access to statement, payment, and related data to customers of the GSA.  Its functions include the following:

 

  • Viewing statements.
  • Printing statements via a PDF viewer.
  • Downloading transaction details as a CSV file.
  • Viewing payments.
  • Viewing refunds.
  • Corresponding with the GSA to report issues or ask questions about VCSS, statements, and accounts.
  • Filing disputes.
  • Viewing information about an account code.
  • Viewing summary information by business line.
  • Viewing summary information by customer account code.

 

Other sources of helpful information:

 

  • Vendor and Customer Self Service User Guide (on the "help" tab at http://vcss.gsa.gov)
  • Customer Training on VCSS (click the link on the "help" tab at http://vcss.gsa.gov)

  • Vendor specific help, through the "help" link within the VCSS application.
